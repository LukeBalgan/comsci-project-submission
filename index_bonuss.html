There is a Solution Among us.

Hi! I'm Luke. I'm a small man living in a small place, but with big goals and big ideas. I'm only just turning 15 but that won't stop me from dreaming. Maybe it's because I enrolled into Pisay. I don't know for sure. What I do know is that I like to mess around and see where that gets me. So I thought to myself, what problem can I solve for today? I thought to myself that I could solve overpopulation, but not in the waay I'd expect at first. I was considering cannibalism, but that would only lead to disease. I also tried a battle royale, but that wouldn't work either because it's too violent and hard to clean up. What else could I do?

That's when it hit me.

Of course! Why don't we just recreate one of the trending games of 2020, Among Us? It's fun, it's intense, and it's thrilling. And, most importantly, it fixes our problem of overpopulation. As a long-time player of this game I couldn't be prouder to use this as inspiration for my new idea. And so I wrote my essay and turned it in.

The problem

Now if there's one big problem with me it's that I procrastinate. A lot. I usually don't end up doing my work until the last few days they're due. Even this project is well overdue, about half a week. So I'm sorry if this is the case, but nonetheless, enjoy the content.
